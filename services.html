FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

How long does a brand identity project typically take? +

A complete brand identity system typically takes 7–14 business days from your brief submission to final delivery. Rush delivery (3–5 days) is available at an additional fee.

Do you offer payment plans? +

Yes. I require a 50% deposit before starting any project, with the remaining balance due upon completion and before final file delivery. Flexible installment plans are available for retainer services.

How many revision rounds are included? +

All packages include 2 rounds of revisions. Each additional revision round is charged at 15% of the total project fee to ensure quality and mutual respect for creative time.

What file formats will I receive? +

You receive all source files (AI, PSD) and final export formats (PNG, SVG, PDF, JPEG) optimized for both print and digital use at all required resolutions and sizes.

Can you work with businesses outside Nigeria? +

Absolutely. I work with clients globally. All project communication is handled via WhatsApp, email, or video call. International payments accepted via USD bank transfer, PayPal, or crypto.
